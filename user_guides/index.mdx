## Key Features:

The first step to world-class documentation is setting up your editing environments.

- AI Summarizer: Instantly summarize lectures, meetings, or long notes.
- Smart Search: Find any note, even by topic or idea, using natural language.
- Task & Reminder Integration: Convert notes into tasks and schedule them.
- Real-Time Sync: Access and update your notes on any device.
- Mind Map Generator: Visually organize thoughts and connections.
